Accessibility

Last updated June 25, 2026

R&G C. Excavation LLC wants everyone to be able to use our website and reach us easily, including people who use assistive technology.

What We Do

We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA as a guide. In building this site we have worked to:

  • Use clear, readable text with strong color contrast
  • Provide descriptive text for meaningful images
  • Support keyboard navigation and visible focus
  • Use proper headings and labels so screen readers can follow the page
  • Respect reduced-motion settings for animations

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and parts of the site may not yet be perfect. We continue to make improvements over time.

Need Help or Have Feedback?

If you run into any barrier on this site, or you would prefer to handle your request by phone, we are glad to help directly. Call R&G C. Excavation LLC at (210) 748-4371 and we will assist you and work to fix the issue.
