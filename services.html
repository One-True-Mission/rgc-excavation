What We Do

Excavation Services

Big or small, we dig it all. From full site prep to a single underground plumbing repair, here is what R&G C. Excavation brings to your San Antonio project.

Excavation & Site Prep

Digging, clearing, and preparing your site for whatever comes next, from new construction pads to driveways. We bring the right equipment for the job.

Sewer & Water Lines

Trenching and installing new sewer and water lines, plus locating and replacing failing ones. Done to code and built to last.

Septic Digging

Excavation for septic tanks and systems, dug clean and to spec so your install goes in smooth and stays trouble-free.

Grading & Driveways

Leveling and grading for proper drainage, plus driveway prep and base work that holds up to Texas weather and traffic.

Trenching

Clean, precise trenching for utilities, drainage, and plumbing runs, dug to the depth and line your project requires.

Underground Plumbing Repair

Stuck with an ongoing plumbing issue? We locate the problem below grade and fix it right, whether it is big or small.
